ABOUT

Signe Arlbjørn

I am a dedicated 20-year-old with a strong foundation in art.

As a person...
I am very responsive, openminded and get well received by the people I work with. Passion, honesty, hard work and loyalty are my core-values. The balance of having fun while working seriously is very important to me. Life is short, so let’s use our time on what creates meaning for us.

As a multimedia design student..
I aim to create solutions and layouts in which the client can reflect themselves, their brand and the message, which they want to push forward, in. Design is not just a result it’s a strategy. Good design can make a crucial impact on a company’s business and role in the value chain. When it comes to digital marketing strategy, content matters and should be given attention.

Finally…
To sum up, the essentials you need to know about me are that I am everlearning, ever-evolving and most importantly ever caring.

PROJECT GALLERY

WARNING

'Warning' is one of my own projects. I have combined some of my own drawings and images and created a small form of animation with audio.

The project has been made in imovie

I WAS NOT READY

The project "i was not ready" is a schoold exercise. For the project we had to create our own small animated story or art piece in Adobe Animate using your own illustrations.

MOVING CAR

The “moving car” is also an animation exercise from school. The purpose was to get to know Adobe Animate and get familiar with the tools and learn how to create a ‘classic tween.’

STRANGER IN THE DARK

The “moving art” project was made at a school workshop (MovingArt Workshop). The purpose of the workshop was to manipulate a piece of art so that it would appear to be moving. I chose the paintings “Reclining Odalisque” (1839) by Francesco Hayez og “Gallowgate Lard” (1960) by Ken Currie.

The programs I used was Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Animate.
